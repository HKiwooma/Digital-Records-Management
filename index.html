Homicide

Homicide occurs when an individual ends the life of another human being, whether intentionally or unintentionally.

View details »

The term homicide simply refers to any act that caused or resulted in the death of a person. This is different from something the decedent did which resulted in his own death, such as falling off a tall building. However, if the decedent had been arguing with a co-worker on the roof, and the two began a shoving match, which caused him to fall off the tall building, the death would be considered a homicide. Still, it isn’t necessarily murder, as circumstances may dictate that it was an accident after all.

Terrorism

In its most basic definition, the term terrorism refers to any act that that is perpetrated for the purpose of causing terror.

Acts of terrorism deliberately target civilians, neutral military personnel, or other non-combatants, with blatant disregard for their safety.
