Terrorism

In its most basic definition, the term terrorism refers to any act that that is perpetrated for the purpose of causing terror.

There is no universal definition as to just what acts are considered terrorism, but it is commonly considered to be an action that causes fear and/or harm for a political, ideological, religious, or economic objective. Acts of terrorism deliberately target civilians, neutral military personnel, or other non-combatants, with blatant disregard for their safety.
